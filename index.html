Our Story & 1-Year Milestone

Born from a
Love for Honest,
Beautiful Food.

Grains & Garden was born from a simple, powerful belief: that Jhansi deserved a dining space as vibrant, inspired, and beautiful as the people who call it home. A year ago, we threw open our doors in Civil Lines with a menu that bridges the globe — and a garden soul that never left.

From our signature Neapolitan pizzas baked to charred perfection, to the silky hummus and hand-rolled falafel that feels straight out of a Beirut backstreet — every dish at Grains & Garden tells a story. And today, we celebrate 365 days of telling yours.
